Vitamin C Powder — Sleep Ritual
How to use
1. Take a tiny pinch of L-ascorbic acid powder
2. Mix with few drops of rose water until fully dissolved
3. After your rose water toner, pat this on face
4. Leave on — do not rinse · wait 10 minutes
5. Apply aloe vera gel on top → B12 moisturizer
Vitamin C stays on skin all night. Only masks get rinsed.
When
— Sleep ritual only — never morning, Vitamin C + sunlight don't mix
— Alternate nights for first 2 weeks, then every night
— Skip on turmeric mask nights — too many actives at once
— Mix fresh every night, never pre-mix and store
Storage
— Keep in a dark, cool place — fridge is ideal
— Always close lid tightly after use
— If powder turns yellow or brown it has oxidized — discard
— Fresh white powder = still active and effective
Watch out for
— Mild tingling = normal, fine to continue
— Burning or redness = too much powder, use less next time
— Never use on broken or purging skin — wait until fully clear
— Do not apply near eyes
